Privacy Policy

Data Protection Protocol

1. Zero-Knowledge Infrastructure

Ikram Cyber System operates on a strict zero-knowledge framework. Personal identifiable information (PII) is processed locally on the user's client-side environment. Our backend modules do not cache, harvest, or store sensitive document data.

2. Sanitized Telemetry

Non-sensitive system telemetry is collected to optimize API response times and daemon stability. This data is rigorously sanitized to ensure it cannot be reverse-engineered to identify individual network nodes.
