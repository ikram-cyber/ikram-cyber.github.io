Privacy Policy

Data Protection Protocol

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Ikram Cyber System operates on a strict zero-knowledge framework for specific proprietary applications. We do not harvest, monetize, or unnecessarily cache your personal identifiable information (PII). Any local data processed through tools like CV Magic remains strictly on the client-side browser.

2. Telemetry & Analytics

To ensure optimal performance and ecosystem stability, we collect anonymized telemetry data, including error logs, latency metrics, and API request routing. This data is rigorously sanitized and cannot be reverse-engineered to identify individual users.
